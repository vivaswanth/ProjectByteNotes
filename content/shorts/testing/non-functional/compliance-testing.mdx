---
title: Compliance Testing
description: Also called conformance testing or standards testing, done to verify whether a system, product, or process adheres to a set of external standards, regulations, or guidelines (industry-specific, legal, or organizational).
category: Non-Functional Testing
order: 1
---
<div>
    | Aspect | Purpose |
    |--------|-------------|
    | Why ? | To check whether the product complies with rules, standards, and regulations (e.g., ISO, IEEE, GDPR, HIPAA, PCI DSS, SOX). |
    | When ? | Typically performed before release (for certifications or approvals) and sometimes at regular intervals (for audits). |
    | Who ? | Done by internal QA teams, external auditors, or regulatory bodies. |
    | Scope | Can apply to software, hardware, networks, documentation, or even business processes. |
    | Outcome | Certification, approval, or clearance for market use. |
</div>
