3. Gayatri mantra - most powerful mantra - vedic mantra - 24 syllables - 8 syllables in each of the 3 padas
    om bhur bhuvah svah
    tat savitur varenyam
    bhargo devasya dhimahi
    dhiyo yo nah prachodayat
    - om - pranava - primordial sound
    - bhur - physical plane
    - bhuvah - mental plane
    - svah - spiritual plane
    - tat - that - supreme reality
    - savitur - sun - source of all life
    - varenyam - adoration
    - bhargo - divine light
    - devasya - of the deity
    - dhimahi - we meditate upon
    - dhiyo - intellects
    - yo - who
    - nah - our
    - prachodayat - may inspire
    - meaning - we meditate upon the divine light of that adorable supreme reality, the source of
    all life, may that divine light illuminate our intellects and inspire us towards the path of truth and enlightenment.
    -  mantra for enlightenment, wisdom, knowledge, truth
    - chant 108 times
    - 3 padas - 3 states of consciousness - waking, dreaming, deep sleep
    - 8 syllables - 8 limbs of yoga - yama, niyama
    - 24 syllables - 24 tattvas - elements of creation
